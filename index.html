Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to DE Community ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile games and this website. By using our services, you agree to this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Our games are designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal or no personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not require account registration
  • Game progress is stored locally on your device
  • We do not collect your name, email, or location
  • Third-party ad networks (like Google AdMob) may collect anonymous ad-related data

3. Advertising

Our free games may display advertisements served by Google AdMob and other networks. These networks may use anonymous identifiers to show relevant ads. You can opt out via your device's ad settings. We do not share personal data with advertisers.

4. Children's Privacy

Our games are suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us immediately.

5. Data Storage

All game data (scores, settings, progress) is stored locally on your device using device storage. We do not upload your game data to external servers.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services including:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google Play Services (app distribution)

These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to clear all local game data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data from your device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of our games after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: decommunity.games@gmail.com
